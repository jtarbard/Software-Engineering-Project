Policy Information

Date Policy

At The Vertex, we take our customer's data incredibly seriously- all of the data on our database is compliment to the data protection act in the following regards:

Fairly and Lawfully processed
-Our data is encrypted using HTTPS and kept on our secure server
Processed for limited purposes/ relevant and not excessive
-All of the data that we process is directly related to assuring that the user books classes and memberships correctly and that the leisure centre staff have all the information needed
Accurate
-All of our data is entered in by the user directly, therefore the accuracy of our information will always be what the user submits to us
Kept for limited time
-All our data is removed after a year of being in our database, unless it is refreshed
Accordance with the data subject's rights
-All user's who submit data to use agree that we have the right to process the data they have entered, this can be edited if requested by the customer under certain circumstances
Not transferred to unreliable organizations/ countries
-All of our data is kept on our secure database and is not transferred to foreign organizations

Any other queries feel free to contact us
