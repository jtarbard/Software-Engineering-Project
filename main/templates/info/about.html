About the Vertex

The Vertex is a large sports centre based in north-west Leeds. Founded in 2000 to meet the needs of Leeds' ever-growing population the centre began with a medium sized gym, pool and outside grounds and has since undergone multiple refurbishments and extensions. The Vertex is now home to a state of the art gym, olympic sized swimming pool, large studio, two sports halls, a tennis court, a climbing wall, and an extensive outside playing field making the Vertex one of the best equipped sports centres in the north of England.

Accessibility & Parking

Located just off the A660, next to the University of Leeds, the Vertex Sports Centre is accessible by car, bicycle and foot. We have our own free to use car park and are in close proximity to the university car park. There are a number of bike locking stations around the sports centre and more can be found around the university campus.

Frequently Asked Questions

Membership/Using the Vertex

How do I join the Vertex?

You can join the Vertex by purchasing a membership via the membersip page or in-person by asking at the reception.

Is there a joining fee?

No, you can become a member at the Vertex anytime with no joining fee.

Is there an age limit?

The Vertex can be attended by individuals of all ages however those under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership via the 'My Account' page under 'Manage Membership' or in-person by asking at the reception.

Activities/Services

Do I have to be a member to access activities?

You don't have to have a membership to attend activities. You can create a free account by signing up at the register page and then you are free to book activities on the activities page.

Do you offer personal training?

The Vertex does not offer personal training however our staff are always on hand to provide an induction, spot you when you're using equipment or to offer advice.

Facilities

Do you have changing rooms and showers?

We have gendered, gender-neutral and disabled changing facilities and showers throughout the sports centre.
