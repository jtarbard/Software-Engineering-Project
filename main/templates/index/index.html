The Vertex Sports Center

The Vertex is a large sports centre based in north-west Leeds. Founded in 2000 to meet the needs of Leeds' ever-growing population the centre began with a medium sized gym, pool and outside grounds and has since undergone multiple refurbishments and extensions.
